Immigrations

Part One

It was a day of late Summer long time ago. My trip to Canada landed at Richmond YVR. I was so thrilled to see how beautiful this nation was. I felt I belonged to this right away.I was invited to speak about auditing and International Financial Reporting Systems (IFRS) at Convention Center near the water of downtown Vancouver. I had a chance to access materials of US Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (US GAAP). Afterwards, Canadian government, the Superintendent of bankruptcy Canada and Office of the Superintendent of Financial Institutions had a courtesy invitation to me to help rebuild a robust accounting structure for them nation wide. I accepted this offer and that is why I am here, in Canada.
